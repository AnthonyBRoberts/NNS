From Nebraska City to Chadron...

Our state's capital has a lot of stories to tell, and we are the people to tell them. The Nebraska News Service offers a unique opportunity to news organizations across the state as well as the students in the bureau. Located in Lincoln, the political heart of the state, we are able to provide our clients with powerful, relevant stories, straight from the source. In turn, our reporters get real-life experience and exposure to the news world. We put particular emphasis on providing content that is relevant to newspapers and their readers across our state. Currently, our stories are distributed to more than 100 news organizations, with a combined circulation greater than 270,000! If you wish to join our service please sign up below.
